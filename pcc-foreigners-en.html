China Police Clearance Certificate (PCC) for Foreigners

Overview · Steps · Documents · FAQ

Who is this for?

Foreign nationals who lived, studied, or worked in mainland China and need a China Police Clearance Certificate (PCC) for immigration, visa, work, or study.

Suggested Steps

  1. Assess: confirm China city & stay period, purpose, and destination requirements.
  2. Prepare documents: passport bio page, China visa/residence permit, registration/proof from school or employer, authorization letter (if using an agent).
  3. Apply: submit to the competent authority; arrange translation & notarization where needed.
  4. Legalize: Apostille or consular legalization as required by the destination.
  5. Submit: provide to the embassy/immigration/employer/school.
Requirements differ by city and destination. Follow the latest guidance from the issuing and destination authorities.
