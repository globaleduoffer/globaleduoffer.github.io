China Police Clearance Certificate (PCC) – Complete Overview

A China Police Clearance Certificate (PCC), also known as a China Police Certificate or No Criminal Record Certificate, is an official document issued by the Public Security Bureau confirming whether an individual has a criminal record during their stay in China. It is commonly required for immigration, permanent residency (PR), work visas, and overseas employment.

Many former residents of China face challenges when applying from overseas due to varying city regulations, notarization requirements, and legalization procedures. Our team coordinates with local authorities across major cities including Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou and Shenzhen to ensure full compliance with immigration standards.
