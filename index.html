Why Many Immigration Applicants Start with China PCC

A China Police Clearance Certificate (PCC), also known as a China Police Certificate or No Criminal Record Certificate, is an official document issued by the Public Security Bureau confirming whether an individual has a criminal record during their stay in China. It is commonly required for immigration, permanent residency (PR), work visas, and overseas employment.

Former residents of China often face challenges when applying from overseas because city rules, residence records, notarization, apostille and legalization requirements may differ. Global Attest helps applicants review the destination requirement first, then prepare the China-side route accordingly.

Start with our main China PCC service page, or choose a destination-specific page such as China PCC for Canada PR when it matches your case.
