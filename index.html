FAQ

Common questions before you start

Short answers for applicants comparing routes before contacting us.

Can I apply after leaving China?

In many cases, yes. We first check your city, residence history and available records before confirming the route.

How fast can a China PCC be handled?

Many eligible cases can be completed in about 7-10 working days after the required documents are ready.

Do I need apostille or legalization?

It depends on the receiving country and institution. We review this before preparing the final document package.

Can you help with WES and degrees?

Yes. We support WES credential evaluation, China degree verification and academic document preparation.
