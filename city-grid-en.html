China Police Certificate City Guides

Use this hub to find city-specific notes for China Police Clearance Certificate applications. Each guide explains common document checks, remote application risks, authentication routes and related resources for overseas use.

Overview

A China Police Clearance Certificate can be requested for immigration, employment, study, residence, visa, licensing and overseas background checks. The practical route may differ by city because residence records, accepting offices and available evidence are local.

Choose the city connected with your China stay. If you lived in more than one city, compare the requested period with each residence record before deciding how to prepare the certificate package.

FAQ

Do China PCC requirements vary by city?

Yes. The issuing route, accepted residence evidence and practical document handling may vary by city, district and applicant history.

Which city should I choose for a China PCC?

Start with the city where you lived, studied or worked during the period requested by the receiving authority. If you lived in multiple cities, each period should be reviewed.

Can Global Attest help if my city is not listed?

Yes. These city pages cover common cases, but Global Attest can also review other China city histories before you prepare documents.

Do city PCC pages replace official instructions?

No. They are practical guides. Local authorities and receiving institutions can update requirements, so the current route should be confirmed before filing.

Not sure which city applies?

Send your China cities, residence dates, passport history and destination country. We can help review the route before you prepare the wrong document package.
