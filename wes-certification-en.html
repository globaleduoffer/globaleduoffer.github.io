WES Credential Evaluation

Overview · Steps · Documents · FAQ

Who needs this?

Applicants to Canada/US immigration, universities, or employers who require a WES (World Education Services) credential evaluation.

Typical evaluation types

  • Course/credential evaluation for study or employment
  • Equivalency reports for immigration/visa purposes

Suggested steps

  1. Create account: register on WES and obtain your reference number.
  2. School submission: have your institution send official transcripts/degree proof (sealed mail or approved e‑channels).
  3. Prepare documents: diploma/degree and transcripts; arrange translations and notarization where required.
  4. Track evaluation: once documents are received, WES reviews them; timing varies by institution response and queue.
  5. Use results: submit the report to schools, employers, or immigration authorities.

Common documents

  • Passport bio page
  • Copy of diploma/degree
  • Official transcripts (institution‑sent or e‑transcripts)
  • Translations and, if needed, notarization
Note: requirements differ by institution and program. Always follow the latest WES and school instructions.
