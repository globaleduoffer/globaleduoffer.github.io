Testimonials from Clients Around the World

We work long-term with clients who have lived, studied or worked in China, helping them handle China-related police certificates, notarisation and legalisation, degree verification and study-in-China matters. The testimonials below are based on real cooperation experiences; only personal privacy details have been removed or anonymised.

Michael Park
France · Study-in-China Support

I was accepted into a two-year master's program in international business at a university in Nanjing, but the French grading system uses a different scale and my Chinese university was initially uncertain how to assess my undergraduate transcripts. Your team prepared a detailed grading conversion explanation that the admissions office accepted, helped me gather the correct apostille for my French baccalaureate, and clarified the health examination requirements specific to my arrival province. I enrolled without any administrative delays and my residence permit was processed smoothly in the first month.

Russia · China Police Clearance (PCC)

I needed a police clearance certificate covering my time studying in Beijing for a skilled worker visa application in Germany. The complication was that my period of residence spanned two different districts in Beijing, and I was unsure whether one certificate would cover both or whether I needed to apply separately to two bureaus. Your team confirmed the correct procedure, prepared all supporting materials in Chinese, and submitted applications to both district bureaus simultaneously so the certificates arrived within days of each other. The German immigration authority accepted both documents without any follow-up questions.

Your team knew exactly which body to contact, prepared the verification request letter in Chinese, and ensured the response was formatted in a way that WES accepted as a supporting document. My evaluation was returned with a full credential recognition and I was able to proceed with my licensing application.
