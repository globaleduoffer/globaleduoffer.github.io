Real Case Studies & Application Paths

We have selected a number of typical real-life case studies to help you quickly understand what to pay attention to and which issues often arise in practice. All cases have been anonymised and scenario-based; only the process and experience that are truly useful to you are kept.

Overview of Typical Cases

Case: Obtaining a China Police Certificate Remotely While Living in the US

Scenarios: Immigration · Work visa extension · Long-term stay visas

The client had been living overseas for many years and needed to supplement a China Police Certificate covering his period of stay in China. Because the records involved several issuing cities and he could not return in person, he chose to work with a professional team for fully remote handling.

Case: Preparing a Hague Apostille on Chinese Degree Documents for Germany

Scenarios: Work visa · Professional registration · Settlement

The client needed to use Chinese degree documents in Europe. Unfamiliar with the combined process of notarisation plus Hague apostille, we helped confirm the exact document types and authorities and completed the apostille within the required timeframe.

Case: Completing CSCSE Foreign Degree Verification After a Master’s Abroad

Scenarios: Household registration · Civil service exams · Job hunting · Title evaluation

After completing a master’s degree overseas, the client returned to China and needed the official Foreign Academic Degree Verification Report from CSCSE. During material preparation and online submission, we provided full-process guidance and optimisation suggestions.

Scenarios: Non-Hague countries · Work visa · Company set‑up

After obtaining a job offer abroad, the client was required to provide degree and China PCC documents that had completed the full chain of notarisation, Ministry of Foreign Affairs legalization and embassy legalization.
