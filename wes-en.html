6 Steps to Complete Your WES Credential Evaluation

1
Initial Inquiry & Background

Share your highest degree, institution, country of study and your purpose (study, immigration or licensing). We will assess which WES evaluation type and service scope best fits your needs.

2
Confirm Service Plan & Fee

Based on your situation we explain the required documents, estimated processing time and our service options. Once you confirm and pay the service fee via international transfer, we start working on your case immediately.

3
Prepare Documents & Translation

With our guidance you prepare transcripts, diplomas and other required documents. We handle certified translations and formatting, and perform an initial consistency check.

4
University Delivery & Online Submission

We guide you in requesting that your university or archive sends official transcripts to WES, and assist you in completing the WES online forms and document uploads correctly.

5
WES Review & Status Follow-up

After WES receives all required documents, your file enters the evaluation process. We help monitor status updates and provide suggestions if WES asks for additional information or clarification.

6
Report Issued & Next Steps

Once WES issues the evaluation report, we help you review the details and plan how to send or use it for your target universities, immigration authority or licensing body, offering further advice when needed.

Service Scope, Fee & Timeline

Our WES-related services may include: certified translation, document review, guidance for university delivery, WES account setup and evaluation type selection, online submission assistance and ongoing status tracking. You may choose a single service or a bundled package depending on your needs.

The service fee varies depending on your degree level, number of institutions, evaluation type, and the extent of support required. After reviewing your background, we will provide a clear and transparent quotation.

In most cases, after WES receives all required documents, the evaluation time is approximately 7–20 business days. If university delivery is delayed or WES requires further verification, the overall timeline may be longer, and we will explain this to you honestly in advance.

Additional insight: avoiding delays in your WES evaluation

In practice, many applicants run into problems when universities are not familiar with WES procedures, when information is entered inconsistently, or when translations do not fully meet WES standards. These issues can lead to requests for additional documentation, repeated transcript submissions, and significant delays to immigration or study plans.

Practical guidance based on real WES cases

Drawing on our experience across a wide range of institutions and immigration programs, we help you choose the correct submission channels and ensure that each document is prepared in line with WES expectations. By resolving potential issues early—such as mismatched dates, incorrect program names, or missing pages—we help you receive your ECA report sooner, keeping your overall application timeline on track.
