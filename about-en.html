About Us

GLOBAL EDU OFFER LIMITED is based in Shenzhen, China and focuses on handling and supporting China-related compliance documents. We provide one-stop solutions for clients around the world and help them smoothly complete every part of their documentation that involves China.

Illustration of the GLOBAL EDU OFFER LIMITED cross-border service team working together.

In real-life scenarios of cross-border study, work and immigration, many clients face difficulties with China-related compliance documents: how to apply for a China Police Certificate while abroad, how to prepare academic and supporting documents that are recognised by overseas institutions, and how to connect notarisation, legalization and translation procedures across different countries.

Our team has extensive experience in cross-border services and has long served clients from North America, Europe, Asia and other regions. At every step of the process we insist on high compliance standards and strict quality control, so that all documents meet the expectations of both clients and destination authorities in terms of legality, accuracy and timeliness.

We care about more than just the documents

For many clients, a Police Certificate, a degree verification report or a notarised document is directly linked to whether an immigration application will be approved, an offer can be confirmed or onboarding can proceed smoothly.

That is why we always explain the process in a way that is clear, transparent and actionable. Under the premise of compliance, we strive to simplify communication and preparation so that you can focus on the key milestones in your life and career.

Our Core Services

  • China Police Certificates (PCC) for the mainland and extended-use support
  • Hague apostille and consular legalization with Chinese ministries and embassies in China
  • Guidance on WES and other international degree evaluations (for graduates from different countries and regions)
  • Verification of academic records and transcripts through official Chinese platforms (e.g. CHSI)
  • Professional translation of legal and official documents (primarily Chinese–English)
  • Consulting and document support for studying in China
  • Scholarship application consulting and document guidance for Chinese universities and selected international programmes

We support not only individual clients with documentation for study, immigration and employment background checks, but also multinational companies, educational institutions and law firms with batch and standardised document-handling solutions. With years of industry experience and mature internal processes, we have helped many clients efficiently resolve complex China-related documentation issues.

Our Team

Our company is formed by a professional team specialising in cross-border education, international document compliance services and immigration-related support. Thanks to our combined expertise across several fields, we can provide systematic, standardised and trackable solutions for global clients, and proactively identify potential risks in complex scenarios to propose more reliable paths.

Looking ahead, we will continue to deepen our expertise in cross-border education and China-related compliance documents, building a trustworthy, international service brand that offers convenient, transparent and reliable support for users worldwide.
