About Global Attest

Global Attest is a Shenzhen-based document support company specializing in China Police Certificates (PCC), apostille, consular legalization, degree verification, WES support, and cross-border compliance documentation.

We help former residents of China, immigration applicants, international students, foreign professionals, law firms, and overseas businesses obtain China-related documents remotely without returning to China.

We also support foreign founders and overseas companies with China company formation, WFOE setup documentation, business licensing coordination, and post-setup compliance guidance.

Our story

Global Attest was created to solve a common problem faced by people who once lived, studied, or worked in China: after leaving the country, they are often asked to provide Chinese police, academic, business, or legal documents for immigration, study, employment, or cross-border compliance purposes.

The process can be confusing because requirements vary by city, institution, document type, and destination country. We built our service around careful case review, transparent communication, and practical execution through the relevant China-side procedures.

How we work

We use a structured process so clients understand the route before spending time preparing documents.

1. Case review

We review your document purpose, destination country, China history, identity documents, and deadline.

2. Document checklist

We confirm what is required and explain any city-specific or authority-specific requirements.

3. China-side processing

Our team follows the relevant local route for application, verification, notarization, apostille, or legalization support.

4. Final review and delivery

We check key details such as name, passport number, dates, and requested format before final delivery.
