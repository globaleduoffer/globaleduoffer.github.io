China Police Clearance Certificate Samples

Reference samples for China police clearance certificates, China police certificates, no criminal record certificates and China PCC documents.

Overview

This resource explains what a China Police Clearance Certificate sample may look like, how it differs from a notarial police certificate, and when apostille or embassy authentication may be required for overseas use.

Different Chinese cities and receiving authorities may use different formats. Use this page as a reference, then confirm the exact format required by your destination country or institution.

What is a China Police Clearance Certificate?

A China Police Clearance Certificate is a document used to show whether a person has a recorded criminal history in China for a specific period. It may appear as a local police certificate, a no criminal record certificate, or a notarized certificate package depending on the city and destination authority.

Who Needs This?

Immigration and PR applicants

Applicants using a China PCC for Canada PR, U.S. immigration, Australia migration, UK visas, EU residence and similar applications.

Former China residents

People who lived, studied or worked in China and need to show a no criminal record certificate after leaving China.

Employers and schools

Organizations checking whether a China-issued police certificate, translation or notarized version is the correct evidence.

Applicants needing legalization

Users who must add apostille, embassy authentication or certified translation before submitting the certificate overseas.

Sample / Template

The sample below shows a redacted Public Security Bureau police certificate. Other formats may include a notarial certificate, English translation, apostille page or embassy authentication sticker.

How to Use It

  1. Check the applicant name, passport number, nationality, search period, issue date and issuing authority seal.
  2. Confirm whether the receiving authority asks for a PSB certificate, a notarial certificate, an apostilled certificate or embassy legalization.
  3. If the document is not bilingual, prepare a certified English translation or notarial translation when required.
  4. Do not copy a sample format directly; always follow the issuing city and destination authority requirements.

Common Mistakes

Using PCC when a notarial certificate is required

Some immigration authorities prefer a notarial police certificate rather than a standalone local PSB certificate.

Ignoring validity period

Some China PCC documents have a stated validity period, while receiving authorities may apply their own timing rules.

Skipping translation or legalization

A valid Chinese certificate may still be rejected overseas if apostille, embassy authentication or translation is missing.

Assuming every city is the same

Local processing rules, records, residence evidence and document formats can vary by city.

FAQ

Is a China Police Clearance Certificate the same as a China PCC?

Yes. China Police Clearance Certificate, China Police Certificate, China PCC and China No Criminal Record Certificate are often used to describe the same broad document category.

Can foreigners apply after leaving China?

In many cases, yes. The route depends on your former city, residence records, passport history and local issuing authority requirements.

Do I need apostille or embassy authentication?

That depends on the destination country and receiving authority. Hague countries may ask for apostille, while other countries may require consular legalization.

What does a China Police Clearance Certificate sample show?

A sample usually shows the applicant identity details, search period, no criminal record statement, issuing police authority and official seal. Some formats also include a QR code or validity note.

Is a PSB police certificate the same as a notarial police certificate?

Not always. A PSB certificate is often the local police-issued document, while a notarial police certificate is a notarized package prepared for certain overseas uses.

Can I use this sample as my own certificate template?

No. Samples are for reference only. The issuing authority, city rules and destination-country requirements determine the correct certificate format.

When does a China PCC sample need translation or apostille?

Translation, notarization, apostille or consular legalization may be required when the receiving authority asks for an overseas-ready version of the certificate.
