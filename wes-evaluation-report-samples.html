WES Evaluation Report Samples for China Credentials

Reference samples for WES U.S. equivalency reports, course-by-course evaluation pages and WES terms used in overseas admissions, immigration, employment and licensing.

Overview

This resource explains what a WES evaluation report sample may look like for China credentials, including U.S. equivalency summary, course-by-course pages and evaluation terms.

Use this page to understand report layout only. Official WES requirements should always be checked through WES and the receiving institution.

What is a WES Evaluation Report?

A WES evaluation report is a credential evaluation report that compares academic credentials from another country with a destination country's education system. For China credentials, supporting degree, transcript and verification documents may be required before the report can be completed.

Who Needs This?

WES applicants with China credentials

Applicants who studied in China and need a credential evaluation for immigration, study, employment or licensing.

U.S., Canada and New Zealand users

People preparing WES or similar reports for universities, employers, immigration agencies or professional bodies.

Students preparing documents

Applicants who need CHSI or CSSD verification reports, transcripts, translations and account submission guidance.

Document reviewers

Schools, employers and consultants comparing WES summary and course-by-course report formats.

How to Use It

  1. Review whether your target institution needs a document-by-document report, course-by-course report or another evaluation type.
  2. Prepare China degree, graduation and transcript documents according to the required verification route.
  3. Check whether CHSI or CSSD reports must be submitted directly, uploaded or provided through a verification link.
  4. Before final submission, confirm destination instructions for sealed documents, translations and report recipients.

Common Mistakes

Choosing the wrong report type

A document-by-document report may not satisfy schools or licensing bodies that need course-by-course analysis.

Missing CHSI or CSSD verification

China credentials often require official verification reports or specific delivery routes.

Incomplete transcript package

Transcript pages, translation, grading explanations and verification data must match the agency instructions.

Assuming every evaluator is the same

WES, ECE, IQAS and other agencies can have different document rules.

FAQ

Is Global Attest affiliated with WES?

No. Global Attest is an independent document support provider and is not affiliated with WES.

Can Global Attest guarantee a WES result?

No. The final evaluation result is decided by WES. Global Attest helps prepare and coordinate China documents to reduce avoidable mistakes.

Do I need CHSI or CSSD reports for WES?

Many China credential cases involve CHSI or CSSD verification, but exact requirements depend on your education background and WES instructions.

What does a WES evaluation report sample show?

A WES sample may show the credential analysis, U.S. or Canadian equivalency summary, course-by-course information, credits, grades and evaluation terms.

Is a WES sample an official report?

The samples are for reference only. Applicants must order their own report through WES and follow the current WES document instructions.

Does WES evaluate China degree certificates and transcripts?

WES can evaluate Chinese academic credentials when the required documents, verification reports and delivery method meet WES requirements.

Can WES reports be used outside Canada?

Yes. WES reports may be used for U.S. study, employment, professional licensing, graduate admission and other credential recognition purposes when accepted by the receiving organization.
