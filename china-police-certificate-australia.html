China Police Clearance Certificate for Australia

Also known as the China Police Certificate (China PCC), a China Police Clearance Certificate may be requested for Australia visa, migration, residence or background-check files. If an Australian visa, migration or character requirement asks for a police certificate from China, we help former foreign residents apply remotely, check the China-side route, and prepare supporting steps for submission.

Quick answer: In many standard Australia visa or migration cases, you do not need to return to China. We first review your Chinese city, residence history, passport records and Australia document request, then confirm the remote route before you prepare documents.

FAQ

Australia China PCC questions

Check My Case

Can I apply after leaving China?

Yes. Most Australia visa or migration applicants can handle the China-side PCC process remotely through authorized representation, depending on the city and residence records.

Do I need to return to China?

In most standard Australia visa or migration cases, returning to China is not necessary. We first review your China residence history, passport records and Australia document request, then confirm whether a remote route is available.

How long does it take?

Most standard cases can be completed in around 7-10 business days after required documents are ready. Additional authentication or multi-city checks may take longer.

Are fingerprints required?

Fingerprint requirements depend on the Chinese city, local police office and application route. We confirm this before you prepare forms.

Do I need notarization or apostille?

It depends on the certificate format and the Australia visa or migration request. We review whether translation, notarization, apostille or other authentication should be prepared.

What if I lost my registration form?

In many cases the PCC can still be issued. We can contact the local police station or relevant local office to verify or request reissued records where possible.

Can the original be shipped overseas?

Yes. After issuance and any supporting steps, the original documents can be sent by international courier.
